About E.R Cummings

E.R Cummings Global Mentorship Program

MISSION

To provide vertical and horizontal mentoring opportunities for interested individuals devoted to self-development in ethical values, ministerial arts, leadership, and stewardship.

PURPOSE

To develop and resource leaders and leadership systems with ethical values, timeless principles, and spiritual formation.

VISION

To develop a formal, systemic program of apprenticeship for grooming and developing a group of individuals in self-development and discipleship with the purpose of transferring apostolic wisdom, insight, and motor skills to produce Christ-centric character.

PHILOSOPHY

Success through confluence and sacrifice.

MOTTO

Mentoring more relational, less instructional.

REGISTRATION

Registration For Vertical Mentoring: $50

Registration For Horizontal Mentoring: $20

STRATEGY

  • To meet semi-monthly for personal interaction, discussion, heart-knitting relationship, objective lessons, strategic exchange, fellowship, and intensive apostolic encounter.
  • To distribute the program manual of instruction, topics to be treated, and personal assignment to be completed.
  • To provide opportunity for synergism, apostolic networking, interpersonal relation, and practical participation with E.R Cummings in ministry assignments both at home and abroad.
  • To build a library for resource and self-development and training.

GOALS

  • To pick individuals through registration, screening processes, and application completion form.
  • To schedule strategic meetings for the year.
  • To distribute mentoring curriculum.
  • To distribute newsletters and resources to mentee.
  • To provide vertical or direct mentoring to enlisted mentees.

OBJECTIVES

  • To participate in a vertical mentoring program by E.R Cummings himself.
  • To develop the leadership skills and prophetic intelligence of participants.
  • To develop a specific program unique for each individual.
  • To provide a scheme for accountability for mentees.
  • To transfer leadership and stewardship skills to mentees.
  • To design programs based on individuation.
  • To provide a system of accountability and responsibility for mentees.
  • To provide a monitoring and supervising system for the purpose of compliance with biblical practice.
  • To recalibrate the lifestyle of the mentees and reshape their skills.
  • To raise prophetic watchmen, intercessors, and prayer warriors to stand in the gap and watch over walls of the Body of Christ and the nation.

BENEFITS

  • Semi-monthly newsletters.
  • A place on E.R Cummings' personal intercessory list.
  • Receive resources on specific areas of development.
  • Receive a free copy of E.R Cummings' books.
  • Regularly receive a copy of E.R Cummings' itinerary.
  • Receive a list of E.R Cummings' personal private contact numbers.
  • Access to E.R Cummings' personal email address.
  • Receive regular applications to travel with E.R Cummings on local and international trips.
  • Spiritual activation and apostolic impartation.

Soul Saving Ministries

The spiritual core and apostolic hub of E.R Cummings Global is Soul Saving Ministries, a kingdom community of apostolic people based in Liberia, with an extended location in Sierra Leone referred to as Kingdom centered church. The vision of Soul Saving Ministries was originally conceived by Pastor Victoria Cummings with the aim of winning souls to Christ.

His Kingdom Universal

His kingdom universal is an apostolic networking of kingdom communities or spiritual communities throughout the globe. Its aim is to invade the nations of the world with the gospel of the kingdom and the culture of the kingdom of heaven. His kingdom universal serve as a global resource based for the E.R Cummings Global. Through His kingdom Universal,E.R Cummings Global disciples nations and kingdom communities or spiritual communities with a range of apostolic leadership development, strengthening spiritual communities ( churches ) and expanding the capacity of believers in Christ and bringing the ordinary believers into the realm of ministry.

Lincoln Memorial Foundations

Lincoln Memorial Foundations is a subsidiary of E.R Cummings Global, an independent foundation or family foundation headed by an independent board. LMF is a nonprofit organization that supports charitable activities in order to glorify God and serve the common good of humanity. The foundation was founded with endowment money given by Dr. Lincoln E.R Cummings, named in honor of the memories of his father. He operates programs with the income earned from his professional speaking and investing the endowments.

Blessed Hands Catering

F.A.Q

  • What is the mission of E.R Cummings Global?

    E.R Cummings Global is dedicated to unlocking human potential and promoting the kingdom of God and the teachings of Jesus Christ. We believe that everyone possesses infinite potential and we offer personalized development services to help individuals and organizations fulfill their purpose.

  • What services does E.R Cummings Global offer?

    Our services include mentorship programs, leadership development, strategic consulting, and spiritual development. We tailor our offerings to meet the unique needs of each client, empowering them to maximize their potential.

  • Who is behind E.R Cummings Global?

    E.R Cummings Global was founded by Dr. Lincoln E.R Cummings, who serves as the Lead Consultant. Lady Victoria Cummings serves as the President & CEO, Apostle Dixon Brohaward as the Vice President, and we have a dedicated team of professionals committed to our mission.

  • How can I benefit from mentorship programs at E.R Cummings Global?

    Our mentorship programs provide personalized guidance and support to individuals seeking to unlock their full potential. Whether you're looking to advance in your career, deepen your spiritual journey, or overcome personal challenges, our mentors are here to help you succeed.

  • How can I contribute to E.R Cummings Global's mission?

    There are several ways you can contribute to our mission, including participating in our programs, spreading awareness about our services, and supporting us financially. If you're interested in partnering with us or volunteering, please reach out through our contact page.

  • Is E.R Cummings Global affiliated with a specific denomination or church?

    While our mission is rooted in the teachings of Jesus Christ, we welcome individuals from all denominations and backgrounds. Our goal is to promote unity and collaboration within the global community of believers, regardless of specific denominational affiliations.

  • Can I request a customized service package from E.R Cummings Global?

    Yes, we offer customized service packages tailored to meet the specific needs and goals of our clients. Whether you're an individual or an organization, we will work closely with you to develop a personalized plan that aligns with your vision and objectives.
